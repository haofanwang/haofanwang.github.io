Biography

Haofan Wang (王浩帆) is a computer vision engineer at TAL AI. He graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in December 2020, advised by Prof. Piotr (Peter) Mardziel. Previously, he worked as a research intern with Prof. Bernard Ghanem at KAUST in 2020 Summer and Prof. Xia (Ben) Hu at Texas A&M University in 2019 Summer. He exchanged at UC Berkeley in 2017 Fall and worked as research assistant at Berkeley RISE Lab. He obtained undergraduate degree from Dalian Maritime University and spent time at RealAI, Everspry, Horizon Robotics and Institute of Software Chinese Academy of Sciences.
