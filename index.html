Biography

Haofan Wang (王浩帆) is a Member of Technical Staff at Lovart AI, working primarily on generative and agentic models. He works at InstantX Team for open-sourcing generative models. Previously, he worked at Xiaohongshu and Kuaishou's MMU (Kolors Team). He obtained master degree from Carnegie Mellon University. He visited the Image and Video Understanding Laboratory (IVUL) at KAUST in 2020 and the DATA Lab at Texas A&M University in 2019. He studied at UC Berkeley in 2017 as a visiting student and worked as a research assistant at Berkeley RISE Lab on the Clipper project. He also spent time OpenMined, RealAI, Everspry, Horizon Robotics, and the Institute of Software at the Chinese Academy of Sciences. His highlighted works include EasyControl, InstantStyle, InstantID, and Score-CAM. He was born inYongzhou, Hunan.
